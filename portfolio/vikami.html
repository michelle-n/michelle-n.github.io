Project Details of Vikami

Vikami is a business selling professional nail products. As a software developer, my key contribution was consulting with the business owners to meet their online business goals. Through my work, I developed and deployed an e-Commerce website built on WordPress, using PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS; building the business' online presence and resulting in a 20% increase in online sales in the first month. As well, I implemented algorithmic improvements and customized shipping estimates to meet the to the business' workflow, while keeping scalability in mind.
